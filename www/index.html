About

At this point I have over 20 years of experience with the game, and 12 years of experience as a Dungeon Master. About Role playing games

What Are RPG's?

Dungeons & Dragons, or D&D for short, is a fantasy Role-playing game that takes place within the shared imagination of the Dungeon Master, or DM, and the players.
‍
The DM’s job is to play the role of narrator and referee, creating the setting,environment, adversaries, and allies the players will meet, as well as mediating the rules of the game.
‍
Each player creates a single character to act as their avatar in the game, basing them on their favorite heroic archetypes in literature and film, or creating something entirely new.

D&D's emphasis on personal interaction, creative group storytelling, and indulgence of the imagination makes it approachable and highly adaptable to any group of friends looking to engage in escapist fantasy and slay a few dragons for a single night of adventure.
